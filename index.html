How To Start The Game

What you see on the left/down is the board. It will keep track of game progress for you. First choose if you will be playing alone against computer or two player with other person. Set it with buttons positioned on black info board. Place your signs in turns. First one to fill a line horizontally, vertically or diagonally wins!
